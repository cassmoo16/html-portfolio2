Favorite Movies From 2024

Here are my top 3 favorite movies that I have seen in 2024 (so far)

Beetlejuice, Beetlejuice

This was a comforting nostaglia flick I got to see in theaters with my younger sister. Very funny and called back to the original in clever ways.

Zone of Interest

Had to watch it over a several day period because it was so dark and thought provoking. Terrifying sound design. Relevant to today's political environment

Dinner in America

Probably the most adorable and relatable romcom I have seen in ages. Showed life on the spectrum in a cute, hilarious way
